What this place is

Vila Presente is a permaculture project in the Atlantic Forest. Not a retreat. Not a resort. A place where banana, coffee, açaí, orange and lemon were planted three years ago, where the walls are still being built from tires and earth.

We host flexible stays, from a few days to a few weeks, for those who want to understand how an agroforest actually works, what natural building feels like from the inside, and what it's like to share meals made from our garden.

This is not a performance of sustainability. It's the thing itself. We're not experts. We're learning by building, and you learn with us.
